# Status Definitions

## NEW
Request received but not processed.

## QUALIFYING
Lead qualification in progress.

## RESEARCH_PENDING
Waiting for property or market research.

## RESEARCH_COMPLETE
Research package complete.

## CLIENT_FOLLOWUP
Client communication pending or active.

## ACTIVE_TRANSACTION
Deal under contract.

## BLOCKED
Cannot continue until missing information is resolved.

## CLOSED
Request completed.
