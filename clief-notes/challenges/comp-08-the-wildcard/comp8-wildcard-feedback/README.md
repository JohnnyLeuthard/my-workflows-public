# Comp #8: The Wildcard — feedback package

One file per entrant. Every repo in this competition was cloned and read in full — every brief, rule file, reference file, and script, pinned to the exact commit that was public at read time — and the feedback in these files cites what is actually in each repo, not what the pitch said. Each file carries three things: the read itself, an idea worth naming (something original in your build, credited to you — take from each other's freely, that is what this list is for), and links to the other builds your feedback points at.

Thirty-five of thirty-seven entries were readable — including three that arrived after the deadline (Jon K, Jayden, Al W), which got the same full read and are marked late; the standings are the judges' call, the feedback is everyone's. Carla and Richard: your repos went private or were deleted after the deadline — your files explain what we need to give you the same read.
