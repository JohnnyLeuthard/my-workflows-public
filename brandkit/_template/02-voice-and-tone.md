## Writing Defaults

- Use plain language.
- Prefer short paragraphs.
- Use active voice.
- Say "you" when addressing the reader directly.
- Use lists when they make scanning easier.
- Spell out acronyms the first time they appear.

## Tone Guardrails

- Do not exaggerate capabilities.
- Do not invent proof, metrics, testimonials, or case studies.
- Do not use fear-based language.
- Do not bury important next steps inside long prose.
- {{EXTRA_GUARDRAIL | Do not speak for others — attribute quotes and claims.}}
