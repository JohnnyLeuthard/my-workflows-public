## Logo Misuse

Do not:
- Stretch or squash the logo.
- Change approved colors.
- Add shadows, outlines, glows, or gradients.
- Place the logo on low-contrast backgrounds.
- Recreate the logo from memory or re-generate with AI without approval.
- Combine the logo with unapproved taglines or partner marks.
