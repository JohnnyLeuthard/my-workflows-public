# Brand Kit Workspace

You are in the `brandkit/` workspace. This workspace holds brand identities — corporate, personal, and product — for use across all MWP sub-workspaces.

## Structure

| Sub-folder | Contents |
|---|---|
| `corporate/` | Corporate brand (voice, colors, logo, icons, writing) |
| `personal-johnny/` | Personal brand for Johnny |
| `_template/` | Guided brand creation template — start here for any new brand |

## Rules

- Each sub-folder is fully self-contained. Do not cross-load between them.
- To create a new brand, copy `_template/` to a new sub-folder and follow the `SETUP.md` walkthrough.
- Do not add private assets or proprietary brand content to sub-folders that live in a public repo.

## Entry Point

Read [CONTEXT.md](CONTEXT.md) to route to the correct sub-brand.
