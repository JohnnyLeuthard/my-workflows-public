## About This Role

Wells Fargo is seeking a Lead Information Security Engineer with deep production operations experience running CyberArk Enterprise Password Vault (EPV) in large enterprise environments. This is an operations-heavy, hands-on engineering role focused on platform ownership, incident leadership, compliance, and automation — not a strategy or people-manager role.

---

## Responsibilities

### Platform Ownership & Operations
- Own end-to-end production operations of the CyberArk EPV environment
- Ensure continuous availability, stability, and performance of all CyberArk components (Vault, PVWA, CPM, PSM, CP/CCP)
- Maintain the EPV platform in a secure, compliant, and production-ready state at all times
- Proactively monitor platform health, capacity, and performance to prevent service disruptions

### Incident & Problem Management
- Act as the primary L3/L4 escalation authority for EPV production incidents and outages
- Lead major incident response, coordination, and recovery for PAM-related service impacts
- Drive root cause analysis (RCA), problem management, and corrective actions
- Ensure incidents are resolved within defined SLAs and service expectations

### Change & Release Execution
- Own and execute production changes impacting the EPV platform
- Ensure proper risk assessment, approvals, validation, and rollback readiness for all changes
- Coordinate patching, upgrades, and maintenance activities with minimal business disruption

### Operational Governance & Compliance
- Own and maintain operational runbooks, SOPs, recovery procedures, and support documentation
- Lead BCP/DR planning, execution, and testing for the CyberArk platform
- Support internal and external audits by providing evidence, reports, and control validation
- Ensure platform operations meet security, risk, and regulatory requirements

### Access & Application Operations
- Oversee day-to-day PAM operations: account onboarding/de-onboarding, policy enforcement, credential lifecycle management, safe and platform administration
- Govern and support break-glass access operations (good to have), ensuring controls, approvals, and auditability
- Ensure consistent and error-free operational support for integrated applications

### Monitoring & Troubleshooting
- Monitor and analyze CyberArk logs, alerts, and metrics
- Troubleshoot Vault, CPM, PSM, and PVWA issues across Windows, Linux, AD, database, and application layers
- Coordinate with infrastructure, application, and network teams for dependency resolution

### Vendor & Stakeholder Coordination
- Act as single operational point of contact for CyberArk platform issues
- Engage CyberArk/vendor support for escalations, defect resolution, and fixes
- Provide clear operational updates to stakeholders during incidents and outages

### On-Call & Operational Leadership
- Lead and participate in 16x7 on-call rotation, providing decision-making during critical events
- Provide guidance and oversight to engineers during production incidents
- Drive operational discipline, consistency, and maturity across EPV support

### Automation & Operational Efficiency
- Identify operational inefficiencies and reduce manual effort through automation and scripting
- Leverage PowerShell, REST APIs, and platform tooling to improve reliability and response times
