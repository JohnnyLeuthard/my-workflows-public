# Screening Questions — 2026-06-LeadInformationSecurityEngineer-FTE

Use these questions during phone screens or hiring panel reviews. Behavioral questions use the STAR format (Situation, Task, Action, Result).

---

## Behavioral Questions

**1. Production Incident Ownership**
Tell me about the most complex CyberArk production incident you've led. What was the impact, how did you coordinate the response, and what permanent fixes did you put in place afterward?

**2. Operational Discipline**
Describe how you've built or improved operational practices for a PAM platform — things like runbooks, SOPs, on-call procedures, or change control. What was missing before, and what did you change?

**3. Audit Support**
Walk me through a time you supported a major internal or external audit of your CyberArk environment. What evidence did you provide, and how did you prepare the team?

**4. Cross-Team Troubleshooting**
Give an example of a CyberArk issue that required you to coordinate across infrastructure, application, and networking teams simultaneously. How did you manage the dependencies and keep the resolution on track?

**5. Automation Impact**
Describe an operational inefficiency you identified in your PAM environment and the automation or scripting solution you built to address it. What was the before/after impact?

---

## Technical Questions

**6. CyberArk Component Troubleshooting**
If the CPM stops rotating passwords for a specific platform and no errors appear in the CPM log, how do you diagnose it? Walk me through your troubleshooting methodology step by step.

**7. Splunk & Monitoring**
How do you use Splunk (or equivalent) to monitor CyberArk health? What are the key log sources you ingest, and what alerts do you consider essential for a production PAM environment?

**8. Cloud PAM Operations**
How does managing CyberArk in a hybrid cloud environment (e.g., Azure or AWS) differ from on-prem? What additional operational considerations apply to credential providers or secrets management in cloud workloads?
