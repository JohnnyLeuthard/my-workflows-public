## Starter Guidance

If the project does not have a logo yet:

- Use the project name as text.
- Use the typography and colors from this brand kit.
- Avoid temporary clip art or generated marks in official documentation.
- Do not imply a formal brand identity exists before one is approved.

## Logo Misuse

Do not:

- Stretch or squash the logo.
- Change approved colors.
- Add shadows, outlines, glows, or gradients.
- Place the logo on low-contrast backgrounds.
- Recreate the logo from memory.
- Combine the logo with unapproved taglines or partner marks.
